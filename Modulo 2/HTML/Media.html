Bear

The bear is a large and strong mammal. It has thick fur and sharp claws. Bears can live in forests, mountains, and cold regions.

  • Weight: 100 - 600 kg
  • Length: 1.4 - 2.8 meters
  • Main food sources: Fish, fruits, small animals
  • Life expectancy: 20 - 30 years

Lion

The lion is a powerful wild cat known as the king of the jungle. It lives mainly in Africa and hunts in groups called prides.

  • Weight: 150 - 250 kg
  • Length: 1.7 - 2.5 meters
  • Main food sources: Zebras, antelopes, buffalo
  • Life expectancy: 10 - 14 years

Shark

The shark is a large marine predator that lives in oceans around the world. It has sharp teeth and an excellent sense of smell.

  • Weight: 680 - 1100 kg
  • Length: 4 - 6 meters
  • Main food sources: Fish, seals, smaller sharks
  • Life expectancy: 20 - 30 years
